Our Service

Orthodontic-(Straighting the Teeth)

We have variety of braces to improve patients facial appearance, through orthodontic treatment, problem like crooked teeth, spacing , crowded teeth, overgrowth and undergrowth of the jaws are corrected

Dentures

Dentures are replacements for missing teeth that can be taken out and put back into your mouth. While dentures take some getting used to, and will never feel exactly the same as one's natural teeth, today's dentures are natural looking and more comfortable than ever.

Ceramic-Braces

Ceramic braces are the same size and shape as metal braces, except that they have tooth-colored or clear brackets that blend in to teeth. Some even use tooth-colored wires to be even less noticeable


Invisalign

Clear aligners, also known as clear-aligner treatment, are orthodontic devices that are a transparent, plastic form of dental braces used to adjust teeth.





Growth Modification

We provide functional appliances to guide the upper and lower jaws into ideal relation to avoid future irregular teeth and surgical correction of jaws.

Dental implant

A dental implant is a surgical component that interfaces with the bone of the jaws or skull to support a dental prosthesis such as a crown, bridge, denture, facial prosthesis or to act as an orthodontic anchor.

Root Canal Treatment

Endodontic therapy, also known as endodontic treatment or root canal therapy, is a treatment sequence for the infected pulp of a tooth which results in the elimination of infection and the protection of the decontaminated tooth from future microbial invasion.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Cosmetic dentistry is generally used to refer to any dental work that improves the appearance of teeth, gums and/or bite. It primarily focuses on improvement dental aesthetics in color, position, shape, size, alignment and overall smile appearance.

Gum Treatment

A professional cleaning by your dentist or dental hygienist is the only way to remove plaque that has built up and hardened into tartar. By scheduling regular checkups — twice a year — early stage gum disease can be treated before it leads to a much more serious condition.
If gum disease is more advanced, scaling and root planning can be performed to treat diseased periodontal pockets and gum infection.

Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery

Oral surgical procedures include tooth extraction, removal of impacted wisdom teeth, Orthognathic surgeries for jaws correction, bone grafting , sinus lift's and implant surgeries. The provide all these surgical procedures with an expert team.
